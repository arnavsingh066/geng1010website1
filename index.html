ECOSTACK

Clean plastic, Clean Island, Strong Future

ECOSTACK isn't just another recycling bin, for Saibai Island it's a new way to make plastic waste management more simple, efficient and reliable. ECOSTACK empowers Saibai Islanders to take control of plastic waste. Our stackable bins are here, to transform how the community manages plastic disposal with a sleek and space-saving design. ECOSTACK fits perfectly in homes, schools, and local businesses around Saibai island, we are all about reducing a plastic footprint on our beautiful island.


What's different between us and a normal recycling bin?

1 - Normal plastic bins are always bulky, and take up floor space. Our bins are designed to stack vertically, making the process of transportation from Saibai Island a lot easier and efficient.
2 - Regular bins often accept mixed materials, but on our small island, ECOSTACK focuses on sorting our plastics to improve plastic waste management.
3 - Saibai Island has a diverse landscape full of insects. We know how tiring it can be dealing with them. So ECOSTACK is built with a tight sealing mechanism to keep insects out and waste secure.

✅ What can go in the ECOSTACK bin:

  • Milk bottles
  • Shampoo and conditioner bottles
  • Dishwashing liquid bottles
  • Detergent containers

❌ What can't go in the ECOSTACK bin:

  • Food scraps or general waste
  • Glass bottles or jars
  • Aluminium cans or foil
